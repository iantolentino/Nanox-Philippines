About Nanox Philippines

Pioneering display technology innovation for over two decades

Nanox Philippines stands as a premier manufacturing entity in the Philippines with 25 years of specialized expertise. As a strategic subsidiary of Japan Display Incorporated (JDI), we have established a global reputation for pioneering cutting-edge small and medium-sized LCD technologies.

Our organization excels in adapting to diverse client requirements, offering comprehensive Electronic Manufacturing Services (EMS) and end-to-end manufacturing solutions that drive innovation and operational excellence.
