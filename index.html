ABOUT US

Nanox Philippines is a manufacturing company in the Philippines with 25 years of expertise. A subsidiary of JDI (Japan Display Incorporated). Our reputation spans the globe for pioneering cutting-edge small and medium-sized LCDs.

Adapting to diverse client demands, Nanox Philippines offers electronic manufacturing services (EMS) and comprehensive manufacturing solutions.
